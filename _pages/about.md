I am an Assistant Professor (Tenure-Track) of Science Education in the [Department of Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies Education](https://coe.uga.edu/directory/departments/msse) at the [University of Georgia](https://www.uga.edu/). 

My research aims to support science practices by developing AI-driven systems that capture how students engage in disciplinary processes such as scientific investigation and argumentation. Drawing on multimodal data generated as students interact with these tasks, I construct theoretically grounded indicators of learning that reflect not only what students produce, but how they think and reason through authentic tasks. These indicators serve as the foundation for explainable AI agents that deliver personalized, timely feedback to students and equip teachers with real-time, actionable insights to inform instructional decisions.

## Research Interests
- Multimodal and interactive formative assessment
- Scientific argumentation
- Scientific inquiry
- Multimodal learning analytics
