I am an Assistant Professor (Tenure-Track) of AI and Science Education in the [Department of Mathematics, Science, and Social Studies Education](https://coe.uga.edu/directory/departments/msse) at the [University of Georgia](https://www.uga.edu/). 

My research advances the assessment and support of science and engineering practices by developing AI-driven systems that capture how students engage in disciplinary processes such as argumentation, scientific investigation, and evidence-based reasoning. Drawing on multimodal data generated as students interact with these tasks, I construct theoretically grounded indicators of learning that reflect not only what students produce, but how they think and reason through authentic tasks. These indicators serve as the foundation for explainable AI agents that deliver personalized, timely feedback to students and equip teachers with real-time, actionable insights to inform instructional decisions.

## Research Interests
- AI-driven assessment of science and engineering practices
- Multimodal learning analytics
- Explainable AI for personalized feedback
- Adaptive learning systems
- Scientific argumentation and evidence-based reasoning
